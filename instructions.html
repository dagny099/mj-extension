How to Use Midjourney Image Tracker

This extension helps you bookmark and organize Midjourney-generated images. It's designed to be simple and unobtrusive, appearing only when you need it.

Tip: If you don't see a bookmark button, try hovering directly over the image for 1-2 seconds. If it still doesn't appear, the image might not be from Midjourney's CDN.

How to Bookmark Images

  1. Hover over any Midjourney image to reveal the bookmark button (üîñ)
  2. Click the bookmark button to save the image (it will change to a checkmark ‚úì)
  3. To remove a bookmark, click the checkmark again

How to View Your Bookmarks

  1. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. You'll see a list of all your bookmarked images
  3. Click "Open as Gallery in New Tab" to see a visual gallery of your bookmarks

How to Export Your Bookmarks

You have two export options:

  • Export as HTML Gallery: Click "Open as Gallery in New Tab" and then "Download This Page" to save an interactive HTML file you can view offline.
  • Export as Text File: Click "Export URLs in Text File" to save just the URLs as a plain text file.

Both export options include timestamps in the filenames for easy organization.

  • Clear all bookmarks by clicking the "Clear All" button (requires confirmation)
Note: Your bookmarks are stored locally in your browser and are not shared with anyone else.
